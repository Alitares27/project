Overview

Site Name: EcoShop

I chose the name EcoShop because it is the union of 2 simple and easy to remember words, Eco is a widely recognized abbreviation for everything related to ecology and the environment and Shop is a universal word that indicates that it is a store.

Site Purpose

The purpose is to Sell eco-friendly and sustainable products and educate and inspire visitors about an eco-friendly lifestyle through informative content.

Scenarios

How do I know if a product is truly sustainable?

All our products go through a rigorous certification process that evaluates:

  • Materials and production origin
  • Manufacturing process
  • Carbon footprint
  • Packaging and distribution

What recycling programs do you offer for end-of-life products?

We have a comprehensive take-back and recycling program that includes:

  • Free product pick-up
  • Discounts on future purchases for recycling
  • Detailed information on the recycling process

How can I verify the environmental impact of my purchase?

Each product includes:

  • Carbon footprint calculator
  • Environmental certifications
  • Environmental impact report
